Baked Potatoes

Description

It's the best to serve it with some meat you love,

may it be a grilled chicken from our recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g of potatoes
  • your favorite spices, but be sure to include:
    • sweet pepper
    • thyme
  • olive oil

Steps:

  1. Cut the potatoes into quarters
  2. Marinate the potatoes with spices and olive oil for at least 2 hours
  3. Bake the potatoes for 30-40 minutes in 180C
