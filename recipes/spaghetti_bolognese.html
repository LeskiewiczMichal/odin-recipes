Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Recipe to making a delicous meal.

Once you make it, you will fall in love with it for the rest of your life.

Ingredients:

  • Tomato Sauce:
    • Can of pelatti tomatoes
    • One onion
    • One carrot
  • 500g of minced meat
  • 400g of spaghetti pasta
  • olive oil (just enough to cook the meat)

Steps:

  1. Chop carrot and onion into little pieces
  2. Fry the meat on olive oil
  3. When the meat is nearly done, add vegetables
  4. Fry for about 3 more minutes, than add the tomatoes
  5. Cook for an hour on low heat
  6. Serve with cooked pasta
