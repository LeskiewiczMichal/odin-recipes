Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Recipe to making a delicous meal.

Once you make it, you will fall in love with it for the rest of your life.

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce:
    • Can of pelatti tomatoes
    • One onion
    • One carrot
  • 500g of minced meat
  • 400g of spaghetti pasta
  • olive oil (just enough to cook the meat)
