Grilled Chicken

Description

You can make it at home with an electric grill

or grill with your friends near the lake.

Either way, it's a great meal that tastes the best with a bear.

Ingredients:

  • 500g of chicken breast
  • potatoes
  • spices
    • sweet pepper
    • oregano
    • cayenne pepper
    • salt
  • mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Marinate cut chiken breast in the combination of mayo and spices
  2. Let it sit in the fridge for about 4 hours
  3. Grill it untill it's 73C hot inside
  4. Serve with baked chicken from our anouther recipe
